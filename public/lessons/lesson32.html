üß† What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. AI systems can learn, reason, plan, perceive, and even understand natural language. The goal is to build systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence.

üß© Branches of AI

  • Machine Learning: Systems that learn from data (e.g. predictions, patterns).
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Understanding and generating human language.
  • Computer Vision: Interpreting and analyzing images and videos.
  • Robotics: Machines that perform tasks physically in the world.
  • Expert Systems: AI systems that make decisions like a human expert.
