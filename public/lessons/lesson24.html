Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks aim to access, steal, or destroy sensitive information or disrupt operations.

  • Malware: Malicious software (viruses, worms, trojans)
  • Phishing: Fake emails/websites tricking users into revealing data
  • DDoS Attacks: Overloading servers to make them crash
  • Ransomware: Encrypts files and demands payment

üîß Key Cybersecurity Measures

  • Firewalls: Monitor and control network traffic
  • Antivirus Software: Detect and remove malware

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Adds an extra security layer
  • Regular Software Updates: Patch known vulnerabilities
