Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines. It allows computers to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and visual perception.

  • Machine Learning: Algorithms that allow systems to learn from data and improve automatically.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Enables computers to understand and respond to human language.
  • Computer Vision: Teaches machines to interpret and understand visual information.
  • Robotics: Designing machines that perform tasks in the real world.
