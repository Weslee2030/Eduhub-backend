Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money, or interrupting business operations.

  • Encryption: Secures data by converting it into unreadable code
  • 2FA: Two-Factor Authentication for added account protection
