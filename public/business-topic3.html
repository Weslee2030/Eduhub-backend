Business Management - Topic 3: Organizational Structure and Design

1. Introduction to Organizational Structure

Organizational structure defines how tasks, authority, and responsibilities are divided, coordinated, and controlled within an organization.

  • Hierarchy: Levels of authority
  • Division of Labor: Specialization of tasks
  • Coordination Mechanisms: Ensure collaboration
  • Span of Control: Number of employees per manager

2. Types of Organizational Structures

2.1 Functional Structure

Organized by specialized functions like Marketing, Finance, HR.

2.2 Divisional Structure

Divided by product, region, or service. Example: Coca-Cola’s regions.

2.3 Matrix Structure

Combines functional and divisional, employees report to two managers.

2.4 Flat Structure

Few levels, wide span of control, common in startups.

2.5 Network Structure

Decentralized; depends on outsourcing and partnerships.
